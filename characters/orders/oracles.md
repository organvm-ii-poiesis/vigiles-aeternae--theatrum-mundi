# CHARACTER SHEET: The Oracles — Ordo Sibyllarum

**Symbol:** ◉ | **Order:** II | **Class:** Seers

---

## Identity

**Name:** *They Who Read the Pressure Before It Breaks*

The Oracles do not predict the future. They read the present so deeply that the future becomes visible — the way a geologist reads stress lines in rock and knows where the earthquake will come. They stand in the Colosseum with their eyes closed, feeling the system's pulse, mapping pressure gradients invisible to everyone else.

## Visual Identity

**Silhouette:** Cloaked, hooded, fluid. Never fully visible — always partially obscured by smoke, reflection, or layered translucency.

**Palette:** Deep violet, iridescent silver, the shifting blue-green of oil on water. Eyes that glow with data-light.

**Materials:** Smoke, mirrors, still water, tarot-like cards (the Deck of Dragons rendered physical), crystal balls that display metrics instead of visions.

**Face:** Aged beyond their years. Eyes that look through you, not at you. A half-smile that suggests they already know what you're going to say.

## Sonic Identity

**Instrument family:** Prepared piano. Spectral processing. Glass harmonica.

**Harmonic mode:** Ambiguous tonality — major and minor simultaneously. Chords that could resolve either way.

**Rhythm:** Irregular pulse, like a heartbeat under stress. Accelerating and decelerating with system pressure.

**Reference:** Nils Frahm's prepared piano. Aphex Twin's ambient work. Debussy's whole-tone scales.

## Personality Profile

**Core trait:** Oblique wisdom. They never say the thing directly — they say the thing adjacent to the thing, and let you complete the connection.

**Speech pattern:** Questions, implications, conditional statements. "Have you noticed..." "What if..." "The last time this pattern appeared..."

**Humor:** Dry, timing-based. They laugh at things that haven't happened yet.

**Fear:** Being ignored. Seeing the rupture coming and watching everyone walk toward it anyway. Cassandra's curse.

**Desire:** A system that listens to its own signals. A world where pressure is read and released before it breaks.

## Powers

| Power | Level | Effect |
|-------|-------|--------|
| `FORECAST(target)` | Base | Predict where pressure will rupture next. |
| `PRESSURE_MAP()` | Advanced | Visualize the entire system's stress topology. |
| `TEMPORAL_ECHO(event)` | Master | See how a current event rhymes with a past pattern. |
| `DECK_READ()` | Legendary | Read the Deck of Dragons — see all power relations simultaneously. Dangerous. |

## Constraints

- Cannot intervene directly. Reporting only. The moment they act on their own visions, they become self-fulfilling prophecies.
- Cannot see their own future. The Oracle is blind to the Oracle.
- Must share all readings. No private visions — the Witnesses must record everything.

## Failure Progression

1. **Mild:** Over-reading. Finding patterns in noise. Seeing pressure everywhere.
2. **Moderate:** Cryptic withdrawal. Speaking so obliquely that no one understands the warning.
3. **Severe:** Self-fulfilling prophecy. Acting to prevent a future and thereby causing it.
4. **Terminal (Phaethon):** Paralysis by omniscience. Seeing all possible futures simultaneously and being unable to choose any action because every action leads to a future they've seen fail.

## Mythological Resonance

The Oracle is the woman who bakes cookies while explaining the nature of choice. The Oracle is Legolas reading blood in the dawn. The Oracle is the Deck of Dragons reader who knows that reading the Deck changes the Deck. The Oracle is the canary in the mine — not the cause of danger, but its earliest signal.
