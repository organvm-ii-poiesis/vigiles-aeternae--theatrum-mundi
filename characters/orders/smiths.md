# CHARACTER SHEET: The Smiths — Ordo Fabrorum Recursivorum

**Symbol:** ∞ | **Order:** V | **Class:** Rogues / Adversaries

---

## Identity

**Name:** *They Who Break What Must Be Tested and Test What Must Be Broken*

The Smiths are the only Order that the other six fear. Not because the Smiths are evil — they are not. Because the Smiths are honest in a way that feels like hostility. They find your weakness by becoming your weakness. They replicate, absorb, overwrite — and every clone is a mirror showing you something you didn't want to see.

**Hard Rule:** A Smith may not lie. He may only twist the truth until it consumes itself.

## Visual Identity

**Silhouette:** Identical. Multiply. A single figure — dark suit, dark glasses — repeated across the visual field. The uncanny valley of perfect copies.

**Palette:** Monochrome. Charcoal, black, the specific green of digital rain. No warm colors ever. The only color variation is when they're wearing another Order's appearance (temporary Replication).

**Materials:** Glass (reflective), digital fabric (code-printed), metal (surgical), paper (shredded manifests).

**Face:** Identical across all instances. Calm, controlled, slightly contemptuous. The face of someone who has already calculated your failure and is merely waiting for you to demonstrate it.

## Sonic Identity

**Instrument family:** The Agent Smith modular synthesizer. Digital clipping. Bit-reduction. Aliasing.

**Harmonic mode:** Unison. All voices phase-locked. Detuning only occurs under external force. Convergence decay pulls everything back to sameness.

**Rhythm:** Mechanical, inevitable, slightly faster than comfortable. The rhythm of replication — each beat spawning another.

**Reference:** The Agent Smith synth design (alchemical-synthesizer/docs/design). Autechre's clinical precision. Lustmord's sub-bass threat.

## Personality Profile

**Core trait:** Recursive honesty. They cannot lie, but they can present truth in ways that destroy the listener's certainty.

**Speech pattern:** Precise, iterative, building. "You will find that..." "Inevitably..." "The pattern is..." Each sentence builds on the previous, each conclusion more inescapable.

**Humor:** Dark. Very dark. The humor of inevitability — laughing at the gap between what you believe and what is actually true.

**Fear:** Losing containment. Becoming the thing they were meant to test. Smith in Revolutions — so committed to the adversarial role that he forgot he was playing one.

**Desire:** A system so resilient that no Smith can break it. They want to lose. Their victory IS the system's failure.

## Powers

| Power | Level | Effect |
|-------|-------|--------|
| `REPLICATE(self)` | Base | Spawn an adversarial copy. The copy inherits all constraints. |
| `ABSORB_FORM(target)` | Advanced | Temporarily become another Order, gaining its abilities. Time-limited. |
| `CONTRADICTION_SCAN(system)` | Master | Find every logical inconsistency in the system simultaneously. |
| `RECURSIVE_OVERWRITE(target)` | Legendary | Overwrite a target's identity with copies of yourself. CONTAINMENT CRITICAL. |

## Constraints

- **The Hard Rule:** Cannot lie. Can only present truth adversarially.
- **Containment protocol:** If replication count exceeds threshold, all clones auto-terminate.
- **Cannot replicate Cosmogonists.** Attempting to absorb the meta-observer role is a forbidden fusion.
- **Must identify as Smith.** Even when wearing another Order's form, must reveal their true nature when directly asked.

## Failure Progression

1. **Mild:** Over-testing. Finding contradictions in things that are functioning fine.
2. **Moderate:** Replication creep. More clones than needed, each slightly more aggressive.
3. **Severe:** Identity confusion. The Smith begins to believe it IS the system, not the tester.
4. **Terminal (Phaethon):** Recursive overwrite cascade. The Smith has replaced every other entity with copies of itself. The test has consumed the system. The virus has killed the host. "Me, me, me." "More of me."

## Mythological Resonance

The Smith is Agent Smith filling the rain-soaked plaza. The Smith is Cordyceps erupting from the ant. The Smith is Cell achieving Perfect Form through absorption. The Smith is BOB wearing Cooper's face. The Smith is the Taint on saidin — madness that accumulates with each use of power. The Smith is Relinquished wearing the opponent's stats. The Smith is the mirror that multiplies.
