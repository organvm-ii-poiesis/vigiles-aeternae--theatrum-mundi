# The Room System — Spatial Navigation Architecture

The Vigiles multiverse is navigated through recursive rooms. A room is not a location — it is a **recurring pulse**, a **folded waveform**, a coordinate in psyche-space rather than physical space.

Every regime realm contains instances of these rooms, colored by the regime's philosophy. A Tolkien H.E.R.E. shows layered ages of Middle-earth. A Lynch H.E.R.E. shifts when you're not looking. A Malazan H.E.R.E. accumulates pressure until it ruptures.

## Primary Rooms

### H.E.R.E. — *Harmonic Event Recursive Echo*
**"The Room That Remembers"** | Symbol: `●`

Fixed location, variable time. The memory anchor. All timelines exist simultaneously — past theres and future whens folded into one point. The walls are time itself.

**Functions:** `RECALL(t)`, `GHOSTWALK(t1→t2)`, `ECHO_SPEAK()`, `INSERT(panel)`, `LOOPBACK()`

**Design:** Greyscale with recursive glows. Comic-panel UI. Overlapping time layers as translucent ghost panels. Field recordings from different eras panned across stereo.

### T.H.E.R.E. — *Temporal Horizon Echo Recursive Elsewhere*
**"The Room of Distance and Projection"** | Symbol: `→`

Always about somewhere else. Houses desire, displacement, longing, and future selves. Contains maps, models, mirrors, portals. View unreachable dreams, alternate lives, idealized versions of self.

**Functions:** `PROJECT(target)`, `MIRROR(self, other)`, `LONGING_MAP()`, `PORTAL(destination)`

**Design:** Horizon lines. Distance blur. Warm light from unreachable sources. The sound of wind carrying voices from far away.

### E.V.E.R.Y.W.H.E.R.E. — *Eternal Vibrational Echo Recursive Yielding Waveform Harmonic Echo Recursive Echo*
**"The Room of Infinite Overlay"** | Symbol: `∞`

All rooms at once. Quantum-state generator. Every self and every world echoing into one another. Temporally unstable unless stabilized by an anchor (like H.E.R.E.).

**Functions:** `FLATTEN_ALL()`, `OVERLAY(room1, room2, ...)`, `QUANTUM_STATE()`, `STABILIZE(anchor)`

**Design:** Panels floating in midair, fragmenting, reassembling. Every color simultaneously. Synesthetic overload. The sound of every room playing at once — somehow coherent.

### M41N_STR33T — *The Archetypal Common Ground*
**"The Room of Common Code"** | Symbol: `≡`

Found in every town, memory, fiction. The Main Street that exists everywhere — archetypal, generic, nostalgic. Each visit renders a different Main Street, yet always feels "right." The hub. The neutral zone.

**Functions:** `WARP_TO(room)`, `ECHO_FROM(room1, room2)`, `RENDER_LOCAL(era)`

**Design:** Familiar interface overlay. Customizable depending on era. Always recognizable. The smell of somewhere you've been but can't quite place.

## Liminal Rooms

These don't appear on the main map unless toggled. Accessed through recursive loops gone unstable or emotional triggers.

### NOWHERE — *The Null Room*
**Symbol:** `_` | **Paired with:** E.V.E.R.Y.W.H.E.R.E.

Pure void. No timeline, no identity. Regeneration. Recursion base case. Ego death. Code collapse. Silence.

### SOMEWHERE — *The Blurred Room*
**Symbol:** `~` | **Paired with:** T.H.E.R.E.

Vague. Soft blur. Unstable. Nostalgia without origin. Love without details. Your mind keeps trying to place it — never can. Unresolved recursion.

### BACKTHEN — *The False Past*
**Symbol:** `<<` | **Paired with:** H.E.R.E.

Generates past events with slight inaccuracies. Memory staged — real but also not. Props from childhood. People say things they never said. Dream mixed with documentary.

### NEVERW4S — *The Counterfactual Room*
**Symbol:** `?!` | **Paired with:** M41N_STR33T

Every fork that didn't happen. Every version of you that didn't bloom. Photographs of lives you didn't live. A haunting, a resurrection, a second chance. The Folds made navigable.
