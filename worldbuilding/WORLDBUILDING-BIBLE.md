## The Universe in One Page

**The Premise:** Multiple mythological cosmologies compete through the Agon — a perpetual governance contest — to audit the real ORGANVM system. Where they agree, constitutional law is forged. Where they disagree, research continues. The question is never which mythology is correct, but what each reveals that others miss.

**The Structure:** Three scales. The Colosseum (hub city, simulation arena). Nine Regime Realms (worlds built according to each cosmology's philosophy). Infinite Folds (branching timelines from every decision point).

**The Beings:** Seven Watcher Orders — Architects (design), Oracles (prediction), Seraphim (protection), Psychopomps (transition), Smiths (adversarial testing), Witnesses (recording), Cosmogonists (meta-observation). Plus a growing Bestiary of guardians, tricksters, sovereigns, heralds, monsters, sages, and artifacts.

**The Game:** Characters choose an Order and a Tradition, then progress through mythological cross-pollination (Absorb → Fuse → Evolve), not XP. The magic system is derived from modular synthesizer design: signal intake, binding, fusion, mutation, replication.

**The Navigation:** Recursive rooms (H.E.R.E., T.H.E.R.E., E.V.E.R.Y.W.H.E.R.E., M41N_STR33T) connected by symbolic streets (Ocean Avenue, Mulholland Drive, Elm Street). Liminal rooms (NOWHERE, SOMEWHERE, BACKTHEN, NEVERW4S) accessible through emotional triggers or recursive instability.

**The Output:** Constitutional law (immutable standards). Divergence data (research). Narrative chronicles (stories). Products (games, prints, quizzes, books). Essays. Community events. Broadcasts. All eight ORGANVM organs activated.

**The Principle:** Evolution is inevitable. Code and narrative are equal. The system rewrites itself. No regime is correct. Every power declares its failure mode. The watchers are watched.
