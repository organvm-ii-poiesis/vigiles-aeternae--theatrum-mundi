# Fusion Rules — Cross-Tradition Power Hybridization

## Prerequisites

You can only fuse powers from traditions you have **studied** (Absorbed). The research corpus gates gameplay — no shortcuts.

| Absorption Level | Study Required | Fusion Stability |
|-----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Surface | Read the corpus summary | Instability 7-9 (volatile) |
| Working | Study 3+ source documents | Instability 4-6 (manageable) |
| Deep | Complete comparative analysis | Instability 1-3 (vibrating) |
| Mastery | Contribute new research to corpus | Instability 0 (stable) |

## Forbidden Fusions (Phaethon Combinations)

The Cosmogonists declare these fusions catastrophic:

| Fusion | Why Forbidden | What Would Happen |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| Smith + Cosmogonist | Testing function gains meta-perspective | A tester that can rewrite the rules of testing — containment impossible |
| Tolkien + Smith | Mandate enforcement gains recursive replication | Infinite copies of the law, each slightly more rigid — ossification cascade |
| Lynch + One Piece | Dream logic meets distributed truth | No one can distinguish truth from dream — epistemological collapse |
| All nine simultaneously | Total fusion | Identity death — no lens remains distinct. The Agon ends. |

Attempting a forbidden fusion triggers a **Phaethon Warning** and immediate Cosmogonist intervention.

## Instability Management

Fused powers have a **mutation rate** that must be actively managed:

```
mutation_rate = base_instability * (1 - study_depth) * fusion_count
```

- Each additional fusion stacks instability multiplicatively
- Evolution (Agon cycle completion) reduces mutation rate by 30%
- Failure to manage: at instability 10, the power consumes the wielder

## Fusion Products

When stable, a fusion produces a **hybrid regime** — a new governance philosophy that neither parent tradition anticipated. These become playable regimes in their own right, expanding the Colosseum's roster.

The first hybrid regimes will emerge from the research phase when deep comparative analysis reveals natural convergences between traditions.
