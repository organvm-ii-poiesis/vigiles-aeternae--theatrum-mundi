# The Magic System — Absorb / Fuse / Evolve / Replicate / Relinquish

Derived from the alchemical-synthesizer creature framework (ORGAN-II). These are not decorative mechanics — they are the physics of the Vigiles multiverse, operating simultaneously as RPG rules, narrative logic, and system governance functions.

## The Five Mechanics

### 1. ABSORB — `signal_intake(source)`

**RPG:** Study another tradition to learn its power. You must spend time in the research corpus (ORGAN-I) before you can use a tradition's capabilities.

**Narrative:** The Protean Hound hunting for behavioral traits. Mnemosyne cataloguing with temporal context. The act of understanding something deeply enough to carry it.

**System:** Ingest a new mythology into the corpus. Translate a foreign governance philosophy into a regime YAML.

**Rules:**
- Absorption requires study time proportional to the tradition's complexity
- You can absorb from any tradition you have corpus access to
- Absorbed power enters your repertoire as RAW — unstable, unrefined
- RAW power has a decay timer — if not Fused or Evolved, it fades

### 2. FUSE — `binding_matrix(source_a, source_b)`

**RPG:** Combine two absorbed powers into a hybrid. The result is something neither tradition anticipated.

**Narrative:** The Chrysalid Siren dissolving identity boundaries. Cell achieving Perfect Form. The moment where two incompatible truths create a third.

**System:** Merge two regime philosophies into a hybrid regime. What does Tolkien+Malazan produce?

**Rules:**
- Can only fuse powers you have absorbed (research gates gameplay)
- Fusion creates INSTABILITY — the hybrid has a mutation rate
- Mutation rate must be managed through Evolve or it destabilizes
- The Witnesses record every fusion — it becomes part of the Chronicle
- **Forbidden fusions** exist (declared by Cosmogonists as Phaethon scenarios)
  - Example: Fusing Smith adversarial recursion with Cosmogonist meta-perception = a testing function that can overwrite the rules of testing itself
- Attempting a forbidden fusion triggers a Phaethon Warning

### 3. EVOLVE — `mutation_constraints(entity, cycle_count)`

**RPG:** Refine a power through repeated use. Each Agon cycle that a power survives makes it more stable and potent.

**Narrative:** The system learning from its own output. The regime that governs and is changed by governing. Majin Buu absorbing and becoming something new.

**System:** A regime refining its audit_rules, priorities, and health_model after an Agon cycle. Regime versioning (1.0 → 1.1 → 2.0).

**Rules:**
- Evolution requires completing an Agon cycle (you can't level up in isolation)
- Each evolution step reduces mutation rate from fusions
- Evolution is irreversible — you cannot de-evolve to a previous version
- The Witnesses record the evolutionary trajectory

### 4. REPLICATE — `clone_bus(source, count)`

**RPG:** The Smith mechanic. Spawn copies of yourself to stress-test through numbers.

**Narrative:** Agent Smith filling the rain-soaked plaza with identical copies. The Cordyceps spreading through the host. Every clone a mirror — identical but slightly off.

**System:** Stress-testing by spawning adversarial copies of an audit. Run the same check from multiple angles simultaneously.

**Rules:**
- Only the Smiths (Ordo Fabrorum Recursivorum) have native Replication
- Other Orders can learn it through Absorption, but at higher instability cost
- Replicated entities converge over time (the clone bus enforces homogeneity)
- If replication escapes containment (too many copies, too fast), the system triggers Smith Containment Protocol — all clones are terminated
- **Hard rule:** A replicated entity inherits the original's constraints. A Smith clone cannot lie any more than the original can.

### 5. RELINQUISH — `parasitic_absorption(host, target)`

**RPG:** The Relinquished mechanic. Absorb an opponent and wear their stats as your own. You become them — temporarily.

**Narrative:** Relinquished equipping an opponent's monster. The symbiote bonding. Possession as identity transfer. The Vigiles equivalent of walking a mile in another cosmology's shoes.

**System:** A regime temporarily adopting another regime's audit rules to see what they see. The Tolkien regime running Malazan's checks to understand what it's been missing.

**Rules:**
- Only one Relinquished bond at a time (single-source lock)
- Your own identity is suppressed while Relinquishing — you see through their lens only
- Duration is limited — the bond decays over time
- When the bond breaks, you return to yourself — but changed. The experience persists as absorbed knowledge.
- Relinquishing a Cosmogonist is forbidden (you cannot temporarily become the meta-observer without losing yourself entirely)

## Power Interaction Matrix

```
         ABSORB → FUSE → EVOLVE
              ↑         ↓
         RELINQUISH ← REPLICATE
```

- Absorb feeds Fuse (you need raw material to combine)
- Fuse produces unstable hybrids that need Evolution
- Evolution stabilizes, enabling further Absorption of more complex traditions
- Replication stress-tests anything in the chain
- Relinquish provides temporary access to powers you haven't Absorbed yet (preview mode)

## Instability Scale

| Level | Name | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| 0 | Stable | Power functions as intended. No drift. |
| 1-3 | Vibrating | Minor fluctuations. Occasional unexpected behavior. Manageable. |
| 4-6 | Volatile | Significant drift. Power may manifest differently each use. Requires attention. |
| 7-9 | Critical | Power is actively destabilizing its user. Must Evolve or abandon. |
| 10 | Phaethon | The power has consumed its wielder. Containment breach. Chronicle records the fall. |
